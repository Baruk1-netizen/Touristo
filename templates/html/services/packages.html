Nairobi National Park Tourist Packages

Nairobi National Park, located just minutes from the bustling city center, offers a unique wildlife experience unlike any other. Witnessing herds of zebra and giraffe roaming freely against the backdrop of the Nairobi skyline is a sight you won't soon forget.

Half-Day Safari:

Perfect for: Travelers with limited time who want a taste of the Kenyan safari experience.
Duration: 4-5 hours
Activities: Game drive in a 4x4 vehicle, guided nature walk (optional), visit to the hippo pool.
Price: $50-$100 per person (entrance fee not included)

Full-Day Safari:

Perfect for: Travelers who want a more in-depth exploration of the park and its wildlife.
Duration: 8-10 hours
Activities: Game drives in the morning and afternoon, picnic lunch in the park, visit to the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust (optional).
Price: $100-$200 per person (entrance fee not included)

Luxury Safari:

Perfect for: Travelers seeking a high-end safari experience with personalized attention.
Duration: Full day or multiple days
Activities: Private game drives with experienced guides, gourmet meals, exclusive access to hidden areas of the park, overnight stay at a luxury lodge.
Price: $500+ per person

Budget Safari:

Perfect for: Travelers on a tight budget who still want to experience the park's wildlife.
Duration: Half day or full day
Activities: Shared game drives in open-top vehicles, packed lunch, basic amenities.
Price: $30-$50 per person (entrance fee not included)

Family Safari:

Perfect for: Families with children of all ages.
Duration: Half day or full day
Activities: Educational game drives with child-friendly guides, interactive activities, visit to the Nairobi Animal Orphanage.
Price: Varies depending on the tour operator and number of children

Additional Options:

  • Giraffe Center: Visit the Giraffe Center to learn about giraffes and even feed them by hand.
  • David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust: Visit the orphanage to see rescued baby elephants and learn about their conservation efforts.
  • Nairobi National Museum: Learn about the history and culture of Kenya before or after your safari.

Tips:

  • Book your tour in advance, especially during peak season.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for walking.
  • Bring sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses.
  • Pack plenty of water and snacks.
  • Be respectful of the wildlife and their habitat.
