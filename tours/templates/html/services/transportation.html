Transportation Options Near Nairobi National Park

Taxis:

Taxis are readily available in Nairobi and can be hired for a trip to Nairobi National Park. Visitors can negotiate with taxi drivers for a round-trip fare or arrange for the taxi to wait at the park until they are ready to return.
  • Little Cab: +254 722 200 000, +254 733 600 000 (known for being reliable and safe)
  • Safeline Cabs: +254 727 121 212, +254 733 626 626 (offers a variety of car options)
  • City Cab: +254 722 500 000 (widely available and affordable)

Uber:

Ride-hailing services like Uber and Bolt operate in Nairobi and can be used to reach Nairobi National Park. Visitors can simply request a ride through the app and specify the park's entrance as their destination. Uber operates in Nairobi, offering various vehicle options and fare estimates upfront. Download the app and enter your destination for availability and pricing.

Buses:

Several public buses stop near the park entrance, including:

  • Route 125: From the City Centre to Karen, stopping near the park's main gate.
  • Route 111: From the City Centre to Rongai, passing near the park's southern entrance.
  • Matatus: Minibuses with set routes and fares, offering a more affordable but crowded option. Ask locals or look for designated matatu stages for specific routes.

Additional Options:

  • Biking: The park offers bike rentals for exploring its trails.
  • Park Shuttle: The park operates a shuttle service within the park boundaries for a fee.

Tips:

  • Consider booking taxis or Ubers in advance, especially during peak hours.
  • Agree on fares with taxi drivers beforehand to avoid misunderstandings.
  • Download offline maps or ask for directions if using public transportation.
  • Be aware of pickpockets, especially in crowded areas.

I hope this information helps you choose the best transportation option for your visit to Nairobi National Park!
